Escape Rooms

Two exciting escape room challenges for you and your teammates to pit your wits against – Now open Thursday and Friday daytime too!

ESCAPE ROOM – NUCLEAR COUNTDOWN

The city you have lived in all of your life is no longer recognisable after years of war. Gradually everything has stopped working as the city lies in ruins. But one secret bunker has never been breached – until now. Something has gone horribly wrong and the fail-safe launch mechanism has started the nuclear countdown. Armageddon is a heartbeat away.

You and your team have just 60 minutes to find the nuclear control console and enter the firing codes to disarm the bomb. Succeed and the world will live to fight another day. Fail and watch the missiles launch and the world, as we know it, to end.

T-60 minutes. And counting.

ESCAPE ROOM – INVADERS

The city defences have fallen. Invaders are marching from house to house searching for survivors. It seems that there is no escape.

An old map discovered in the attic, shows a secret exit from your house, underneath the fence and out to freedom. It is your only hope.

You and your team have to escape. The house has long been locked up and abandoned, and youn will have to use all of your skills and teamwork to go through the rooms and find the secret door from the dining room. But the invaders are nearing your house and they will be through the door in an hour.


It is a race against time. You’d better begin if you know what is good for you.

ESCAPE ROOM – DAM BURST

You have been chained to the wall under the old city by the rebel army. You can hear the rebels getting ready to blow up the dam and in an hour, water will rush through the city destroying everything and everyone in it’s path. It is a race against time to escape. Luckily one of the rebel soldiers has tried to help giving what clues they have to help you escape. Work together as a team to escape before the dam breaks …
Prices from £15.00 per person for 60 minutes.

ESCAPE ROOM – SPY MISSION

You have been trusted to find and deliver secret documents but someone is aware of your task and is out to stop you. Venture out into the streets of Bishops Stortford solving clues as you go* to find the location of the documents and deliver the documents safely. But the other side have agents in the field and they are closing in. Hurry before the documents end up in the wrong hands and World War Three inches ever closer.
*Note – packed lunch can be included for a break half-way around. A mobile phone will be required.

Prices from £10.00 per person for 60 minutes.

Can I Play?

You will be a minimum of 10 years old to participate without an adult. 8 years and above must be accompanied by an adult. The games can be a little bit scary and the tension high as the clock counts down. Speak to a member of staff for clarification.

The House invaders game contains dust and may not be suitable for asthmatics. Old clothes are advised for all activities.

FAQ

How much does it cost?

From £20 per hour session

When should I arrive?

You must be booked in 30 minutes before the start of a game (or 50 minutes if a Drop & Dine package is selected)

Can I get out if I need the toilet?

Yes. Just use the communicator to advise you are leaving the rooms and one of the staff will help you. We will suggest players go to the toilet before the games starts.

Is it too easy for adults or too difficult for kids?

Neither. The games will be adjusted to make sure they fit every group. The games will be difficult for everyone – but NOT impossible.
