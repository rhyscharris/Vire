Nerf Wars

Your choice of weapons. Uniform on. Guns loaded. Let’s go.

You and your team mates have the Arena to yourselves. Blast away to your heart’s content. But there are many obstacles, nooks and crannies that make the challenge just a bit harder. Watch your back.

The city has been attacked and abandoned. It is time to take sides and fight.

Prices from £9.00 for 30 minutes and from £18.00 for 60 minutes.

The Nerf® Arena is a 60sqm ruined city, and you are armed with only a Nerf® gun. Each player will be given a camouflage jacket, a pair of goggles and a Nerf® gun. They will also start with as many bullets as they can carry. Each game will last 12 minutes, with four games in an hour. Bullet refilling stations will be available. All players will be briefed before the first game of any session.

Can I Play?

You must be between 6 and 106 to play this game. This is NOT a running game. Anyone found running or climbing on obstacles will be warned once and then asked to leave the game. Safety is very important. Goggles must be worn when in the arena.

FAQ

How much does it cost?

£9.00 per thirty minute session

When should I arrive?

You must be booked in 20 minutes before the start of a game (or 45 minutes if a Drop & Dine package is selected)

Is it dangerous?

No – as long as goggles are worn and the rules are observed.

Will there be any other types of shooting activity?

Yes, we are looking to have laser games soon – details will be announced via Facebook.

Can I play with my parents?

Yes! In fact family rivalry is a great reason for having a battle – and for once, biggest is NOT best.
