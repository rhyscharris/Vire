Single Player Virtual Reality

Put on your headset. Choose the game you want to play. And enter the world of virtual reality.

For an experience unlike any other, we give you free play on our top-of-the-range virtual reality glasses and gaming hardware. Even if you have used VR before, you will be amazed at the quality of the experience you are about to embark upon.

Choose from a range of games during your booked period. Try one, a few or all of them – the power is in your hands. And when you have finished? We guarantee you will want to come back again and again.

So either buckle up again or join us in the Outside Inn café upstairs for home cooked food and a range of drinks, and discuss your experiences. You’ll have a lot to talk about.

Prices from £10.00 for 30 minutes and from £20.00 for 60 minutes.

Can I Play?

You must be a minimum of 10 years old to play VR games.

Games played will be appropriate for the age of the player.

FAQ

How much does it cost?

From £9.50 for a 30 minute session

When should I arrive?

You must be booked in 20 minutes before the start of a game (or 45 minutes if a Drop & Dine package is selected)

Can I choose any game?

There is a wide selection of title available. Games will be age specific and you must not attempt to play any games unless you are advised you can.
