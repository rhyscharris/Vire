Welcome to VIRE

VIRE is an activity centre like no other. Come inside and experience exciting single player and group games. Challenge yourself or your friends and family. Race against the clock to win, but beware the consequences of losing.

We are open Wednesday to Friday evening between 4:00pm and 8:00pm, and all weekend. In school holiday time we are open every day. Book via the website or feel free to just drop in and see if there are activities are available.

We also have an outside pub-garden café, inside upstairs. Come and join us for a drink or delicious home cooked food, and feel like you are outside in the sun – even when it is freezing cold outside.
