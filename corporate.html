Group and Corporate Events

VIRE is an exciting and challenging entertainment centre, perfect for organising a range of events and activities, tailored to everyone.

We offer all sorts of exciting activities and corporate solutions for up to 50 people of all ages. Work as a team or challenge each other to be the best. Use your mind and your wits to overcome the odds and succeed.

We have four escape room challenges and many VR stations with a range of games, experiences and challenges to satisfy even the most experienced gamer?

Not a gamer? Virtual reality is a whole new experience enjoyed by everyone – whether standing on the top of a mountain, shooting robots or drawing amazing 3D art.
