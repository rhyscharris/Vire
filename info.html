Parking

The nearest and best parking is the pay and display at Jackson Square and Sainsbury’s, just 5 minutes walk from the centre. There are other smaller car-parks in Bishops Stortford, so parking is never a problem

Public Transport

The train station and bus station are 10 minutes walk away from VIRE.

Equipment

We use top of the range equipment here at VIRE, to give you the best possible experience. We will be adding new experiences and features next year so make sure you are signed up on Facebook – @theVIREzone – to receive news of exciting new activities.

Security

We take the security of our visitors very seriously. Every activity room has secure cameras where we record and store video to ensure that we maintain the highest possible standards. Our staff are DBS checked and are trained on how to give you the best possible experience. If you have any questions or suggestions, please let us know.

Feedback

At the end of your visit to VIRE we will ask you for your feedback and also we will take a photograph for our Facebook page unless you would rather remain incognito! We would love feedback from every member of your party and any suggestions on how we could improve the activities and service we offer. Thank you for helping us make VIRE as good as it can be.
